Margherita

Usually this pizza is made with basil, mozzarella cheese and tomatoes in imitation of the colors of the Italian flag.

Marinara

This is a traditional Neapolitan pizza that is made with lots of garlic, anchovies and oregano.

This pizza is topped with olives, artichoke hearts, half of a boiled egg, prosciutto and mushrooms.
